floG

A networked multiplayer simultaneous minigolf game where players can derail opponents using MapMovers: player-activated hazards and moving obstacles. Good aim and timing are vital to sabotaging opponents whilst maintaining a good score. Because anyone can trigger anything, no one is safe!

I designed the MapMovers system and worked it into level layouts to encourage player interaction and confrontation. I also worked on the networking side, by contributing to adding Steam and IP address matchmaking and synchronising player movement and MapMovers interactions across connected machines.

The Tunnels

A first-person psychological horror walking simulator set in RIT's underground dormitory tunnel system. Players must escape from an entity while trying to find their way back to their dorm room after a long day of classes.

I designed and created levels for the game using audio and visual cues to create an immersive, yet eerie and unsettling atmosphere. I also structured the player's in-game encounters and tasks to facilitate pacing and a build-up of tension and anxiety over time.

TapRacing

An isometric reinvention of the traditional 3D, steering based racing game, where vehicles can only strafe sideways and turn 90 degrees instantaneously in either direction. The chase camera is locked to the same orientation, so being able to differentiate right from left at all times is key!

I designed a simple and snappy handling model to facilitate fast-paced gameplay, and also implemented a checkpoint system to ensure a fair experience. I created everything in this project (except the car model) using Unreal 4's built-in editor tools.

mrbl.

A mashup of the action puzzle shooters Luxor, Bust-a-Move, and Breakout. Players must make trick shots using pinpoint timing and accuracy to clear marbles bouncing around an arena by shooting projectiles of matching colours at them.

I created a colour matching system that ensures players are always given projectiles whose colours match those of marbles present in the arena. To encourage players to make more trick shots, I placed targets in obscure locations throughout levels that are worth bonus points if hit.
